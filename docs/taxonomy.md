# Post Taxonomy

## Categories

### Elections & Campaigns
Elections, voting, polling, endorsements, rallies, and campaign competition.

**Cues:** election, vote, poll, campaign, endorsement, rally

---

### Tariffs & Trade
Tariffs, trade deficits/surpluses, trade deals, and import/export policy.

**Cues:** tariff, trade, import, export, deficit, surplus

---

### Economy, Jobs & Inflation
Economic growth, labor market conditions, wages, inflation, and business activity.

**Cues:** economy, jobs, inflation, wages, growth, worker

---

### Taxes & Regulation
Tax policy, regulatory burden, deregulation, permits, and compliance.

**Cues:** tax, taxes, regulation, permit, deregulation, compliance

---

### Border & Immigration
Border enforcement, migration, asylum, deportation, and entry policy.

**Cues:** border, immigration, migrant, asylum, deport

---

### Crime & Public Safety
Crime trends, policing, drugs/fentanyl, gangs, and local public safety.

**Cues:** crime, criminal, police, fentanyl, law enforcement

---

### Courts & Legal Proceedings
Courts, judges, trials, indictments, rulings, constitutional and legal claims.

**Cues:** court, judge, trial, indictment, constitutional, ruling

---

### Foreign Affairs & Defense
International conflicts, geopolitics, military posture, alliances, and national security.

**Cues:** china, russia, iran, military, war, defense

---

### Media & Public Narrative
Media coverage, press criticism, narrative framing, speech and culture debates.

**Cues:** media, news, press, narrative, fake news, censorship

---

### Other
Fallback when no topical category is a clear fit.

**Cues:** generic updates, ambiguous statements, no strong topical signal
